## Policy Development

### Process

1. **Identify Need**
   - Risk assessment finding
   - Compliance requirement
   - Incident lesson learned

2. **Draft Policy**
   - Research best practices
   - Consult stakeholders
   - Align with standards

3. **Review**
   - Legal review
   - Security review
   - Management approval

4. **Communicate**
   - Publish policy
   - Train staff
   - Acknowledge receipt

5. **Maintain**
   - Regular review
   - Update as needed
   - Track compliance
